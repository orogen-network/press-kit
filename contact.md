## Response-time expectation

- Press inquiries: 2 business days, foundation jurisdiction time zone.
- Embargoed releases: by appointment.
- All quotes from Orogen contributors are reviewed by Marketing and General Counsel before attribution.

## Embargo policy

Orogen-issued press releases are distributed under standard embargo: contents may not be published before the embargo date listed in the release header. Breaking embargo forfeits future advance access.
